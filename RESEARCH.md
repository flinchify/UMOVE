## WEAKNESS ANALYSIS — Current Site

### CRITICAL Issues

1. **No Schema/JSON-LD markup** — Zero structured data. No LocalBusiness, Organization, Service, FAQ, or Review schema. AI crawlers and Google have no structured understanding of this business.

2. **No sitemap.xml or robots.txt** — Not discoverable. Can't confirm these exist on the WordPress site.

3. **Zero alt text on almost all images** — Only 2 out of 46+ images have alt text. Massive SEO and accessibility failure.

4. **Title tag on homepage is WRONG** — "Storage Solutions Perth | Local Self Storage" — doesn't mention containers, modifications, hire, or sales. Missing the primary keywords.

5. **H1 is misleading** — "Storage Solutions Perth – Secure & Affordable Self Storage" — they're not a traditional self-storage facility, they're a container company. This H1 attracts the wrong audience.

6. **Homepage "UMA" branding confusion** — Hero says "Welcome to UMA" but the brand is U-Move. Confusing brand signal.

7. **Only 4 FAQ questions** — FAQs page is painfully thin. Need 15-20+ questions with proper FAQPage schema.

8. **No reviews/testimonials anywhere** — Zero social proof visible. No Google review integration, no customer quotes.

9. **No E-E-A-T signals** — No team photos, no staff bios, no "25 years" backed by specific dates/history, no industry certifications shown.

### MAJOR Issues

10. **Ugly slugs** — `/long-term-storage-perth-2/` has a "-2" suffix. `/on-site-storage-containers-mining-operations/` is way too long.

11. **Duplicate content** — Container size specs are repeated verbatim across 4+ pages with zero differentiation.

12. **Thin content pages** — The 10ft, 20ft, 40ft container pages are basically just a quote form with almost no content. These should be rich, keyword-targeted landing pages.

13. **No CTA differentiation** — Every page just says "Get a quote." No urgency, no pricing hints, no booking calendar, no phone callback widget.

14. **Blog has zero internal linking strategy** — Latest News articles exist but aren't linked from service pages.

15. **No pricing transparency** — Not even ballpark ranges. Competitors who show "from $X/week" will win clicks.

16. **Mobile experience likely poor** — WordPress with heavy images, no evidence of mobile optimization in the theme.

17. **No Open Graph / Twitter Cards** — Social sharing will look bare.

18. **Logo has no alt text** — The U-Move logo image has empty alt="" throughout.

19. **Footer is weak** — Just hours/address/legal. No service links, no trust badges, no "25+ years" badge.

### SEO/GEO Specific Weaknesses

20. **No location-specific landing pages** — Henderson, Fremantle, Perth CBD, Rockingham, Mandurah — all missing.

21. **No "near me" optimization** — No geo-targeted content for surrounding suburbs.

22. **No Google Business Profile integration** — No map embed, no review widget, no GBP posts mentioned.

23. **No llms.txt** — Zero AI visibility optimization.

24. **No comparison content** — "U-Move vs PODS", "Container storage vs traditional self-storage" — all missing.

25. **Blog articles use stock photos** — Not U-Move's own container photos. Weak authenticity.

### Design/UX Weaknesses

26. **Generic WordPress template feel** — Nothing memorable. Looks like any contractor site.

27. **No animations or micro-interactions** — Static, lifeless pages.

28. **No visual hierarchy** — Text-heavy blocks with poor scan-ability.

29. **No video content** — Should have facility tours, modification process videos.

30. **No interactive container size comparison** — Should be a visual tool, not text specs.

31. **Contact form is basic** — Just name/email/type/message. No service selector, no budget range, no delivery location.

32. **No live chat or WhatsApp integration**

33. **Color scheme is bland** — No strong brand identity in the design itself.

### SEO Implementation (from checklist)
- generateMetadata on every page
- Dynamic sitemap.ts
- robots.ts (allow all, including AI crawlers)
- /llms.txt for AI visibility
- JSON-LD: Organization, LocalBusiness, Service, FAQPage, HowTo, Review
- All images: next/image, alt text, WebP, lazy loading
- Core Web Vitals target: LCP < 1.5s, INP < 200ms
- PageSpeed 90+ mobile

### Design Direction
- Modern, industrial-premium aesthetic
- Dark navy/charcoal + orange/amber accent (from their logo colors)
- Smooth scroll animations (Framer Motion)
- Hover effects on all interactive elements
- Parallax container imagery
- Interactive container size comparison (3D or SVG)
- Video background hero
- Mobile-first responsive design with device detection
- Micro-interactions on buttons, cards, nav
- Animated statistics counters ("25+ Years", "1000+ Containers Delivered")
